Candy Crush

Score: 0
Moves: 30

How to Play:

  1. Click and drag a candy to swap it with an adjacent candy
  2. Match 3 or more candies of the same color to score points
  3. Special candies are created when you match 4 or more candies
  4. You have 30 moves to score as many points as possible
  5. Match candies to create special combinations and score more points!

Special Candies:

Striped (Match 4)
Wrapped (Match 5)

Scoring:

  • 3 candies = 30 points
  • 4 candies = 60 points
  • 5 candies = 100 points
  • Special combinations = Bonus points!
